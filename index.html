The land of empty houses

+52% of unoccupied dwellings in Italy from 2011 and 2019

Empty houses were 7 million in 2011 and they surpassed the 11.2 units in 2019 (last available data). Rome and Turin are the provinces with most unoccupied houses, with over 350 thousand dwellings with no one inside.
